About Yaw Tutu

Born in Ghana and initiated into Tantra from birth, Yaw has been exploring the healing arts for more than three decades. At 25 he became a Reiki Master and he has spent over a decade training in Thai massage and allied modalities【46223366558556†L62-L68】. His work integrates ancestral wisdom with modern somatic science to support profound transformation.

Yaw meets every person exactly where they are – sensing blockages, imbalances and untapped potential and selecting the most effective combination of hands‑on techniques, energy transmission, movement, sound and guided visualization【46223366558556†L56-L72】.
