About Yaw

Yaw Tutu is a seasoned healer, educator and facilitator devoted to holistic well‑being. With roots in Thai massage, Reiki, Qigong, Tantric Thai massage and other therapeutic modalities, he integrates ancestral wisdom, energy healing and bodywork to create transformative experiences for his clients. His approach is deeply intuitive and personalized, attuning to each client’s physical, energetic and emotional needs. Rather than following a rigid formula, he senses blockages, imbalances and untapped potential, selecting the most effective combination of hands‑on techniques, energy transmission, movement, sound and guided visualization.

Yaw is a Certified Supreme Science Qigong Level 3 Teacher, Reiki Master, MPS Therapy Practitioner, Scar Release Therapist and Licensed Massage Therapist (LMT #MA69685). His work extends beyond individual sessions – he teaches and empowers others with knowledge of bodywork, energy healing and self‑care practices. Bringing expressive arts, movement, entrainment and sound therapy into his sessions, he guides people toward greater alignment, vitality and wholeness.

All bodies and identities are warmly welcomed. Sessions can be booked in New York City, Gainesville (FL), Smithfield (RI) and Lincoln (MA).
