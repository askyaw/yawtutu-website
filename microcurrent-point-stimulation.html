Microcurrent Point Stimulation (MPS)

Microcurrent Point Stimulation (MPS) is a cutting‑edge technique that uses gentle microcurrents to target pain, scar tissue and areas of tension, supporting your body’s natural healing processes【46223366558556†L93-L95】. The device delivers low‑level electrical impulses to acupuncture and trigger points, helping to reset your nervous system and reduce inflammation.

Benefits

  • Reduces chronic and acute pain
  • Softens scar tissue and adhesions
  • Improves circulation and lymph flow
  • Enhances range of motion and mobility
