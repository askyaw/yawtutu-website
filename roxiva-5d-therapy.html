roXiva 5D Therapy

roXiva 5D Therapy combines synchronized light patterns, immersive soundscapes and subtle vibrational frequencies to induce deep meditative and psychedelic‑like states. This multi‑sensory journey entrains your brainwaves into alpha, theta or gamma patterns, helping you access expanded awareness, creativity and profound relaxation.

Benefits

  • Deeply relaxes the nervous system
  • Supports meditation, visualization and creative insight
  • Promotes mental clarity and emotional balance
  • May assist in releasing stored trauma and stress

Session lengths & pricing

roXiva sessions can be booked on their own or layered into bodywork treatments. Pricing is per minute, allowing you to design your ideal journey.

  • 15 minutes – $105 ($7/min)
  • 30 minutes – $210
  • 45 minutes – $315
  • Add‑on to bodywork – $7 per minute

As you lie back with eyes closed, the roXiva device projects gentle strobes through your eyelids while binaural beats and healing frequencies guide your mind. Clients often report visionary imagery, deep calm and a sense of reset.
